What is Codon?

Learn and Build Together

A large number ofonline resouces have been made available for learning, building and collaborating on software projects with people from all around the world. However, it is a daunting challenge for less experienced developers and those looking for collaborators in software projects to fully make use of these tools. Codonis a social approach to software engineering for getting people to learn new skills and build awesome things together.

Something For Everyone

Software development involves a lot of different skills. Database administration, server-side programming, and graphical design are examples of many roles involved in making applications. In addition, Codonis a community for beginners to learn programming and application development and for veterans to teach and lead teams. Codoncreates valuable experiences for all skill levels and spectrum of interests.

Discover and Create New Projects

Using the best social media, learning, and programming tools available today, Codonis the central hub to make and join teams in building things that you care about. Stop by our community on the Codonsubreddit or GitHub page to see what people are working on.
